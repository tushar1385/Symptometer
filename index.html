Connecting to virtual care because we care

The pandemic has affected many healthcare providers’ operating hours and walk-in visit policies. Yet, people need a way to continue getting medical care from the comfort and safety of their own homes. Since the beginning of the pandemic, we’ve seen interest in virtual care and telehealth rise dramatically. Therefore, to help individuals and health care providers connect, we’re focused on providing individuals with access to high-quality and authoritative information and supporting them throughout their health journey.

Anytime, Anywhere


We’re beginning to roll out new features that make it easier for people to connect to virtual healthcare options, whether it’s to a doctor’s office down the street, the hospital across town, or a national telehealth platform. People can book an online appointment for themselves and schedule it according to their comfort. On our end we will ensure a speedy and uninterrupted service for our customers.

It’s all technology

Healthcare providers like hospitals, doctors, and mental health professionals can enter a virtual care offering in their Business Profile. We will be using remote health monitoring technology which will help a healthcare provider access real-time patient data even when it is not under the provider’s geographical control. This technology uses different remote monitoring tools and software to gather patient data and deliver it to the healthcare provider. A patient’s data becomes accessible to every doctor within a matter of seconds. Thus, it is easier to get a person’s medical history for further investigation or on-going medical assistance.
